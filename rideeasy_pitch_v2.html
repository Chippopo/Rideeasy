02 / 07 — The Problem

Every day, ₦50 change costs everyone far more

⏱️
3–5 min

Average delay per trip due to change disputes. That's a missed lecture intro. Lost income for the driver.

💸
Daily

Students overpay because they're rushing. Drivers turn away riders with large bills. Revenue lost on both sides.

🚨
Risk

Drivers carrying day-long cash earnings are security targets. Students pulling wallets in public too.

"A keke that should be moving to the next stop is instead parked, while the driver and passenger argue over change. This isn't inconvenient — it's a system failure happening dozens of times a day."

04 / 07 — The Solution

Scan. Ride. Go.

01
Student loads wallet

Tops up RideEasy app via bank transfer or USSD. Any amount. No card needed.

02
App pre-generates offline tokens

While connected, app creates cryptographically signed ₦200 tokens which is later turned into a QR code and stored locally. No internet needed after.

03
Student boards, scans

Opens app → taps Pay Ride → scanner reads QR token. Fully offline-capable.

04
Token consumed. Keke moves.

Scanner marks token used. Balance syncs to server when internet returns. Zero delay. Zero dispute.
